Yogurt

take down Michael Weston at his own game

Ingredients

  • milk
  • yogurt starter
  • jar

Steps

  1. heat milk
  2. cool milk
  3. add yogurt starter
  4. put into jar
  5. stir
  6. incubate 7-9 hrs
  7. chill in fridge (not you, the yogurt)
